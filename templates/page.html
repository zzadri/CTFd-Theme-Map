Keep the event fair, keep the infrastructure safe, and stay within the expected scope. Edit the CTFd page with route rules if you want to replace or extend these notes.

Fair Play

  • Play with your own account only.
  • Do not share flags, solutions, or private challenge material during the event.
  • Respect other players, staff, and event infrastructure.

Allowed Scope

  • Interact only with infrastructure and services explicitly provided for the CTF.
  • Attack only the targets that are part of the event.
  • Report unintended exposures or platform issues to the organizers.

Forbidden Actions

  • No denial of service, brute-force flooding, or disruption of the platform.
  • No attacks against accounts, teams, or machines outside the CTF scope.
  • No sabotage of challenge availability for other participants.

Flags And Accounts

  • Submit flags in the exact expected format.
  • Do not attempt to bypass authentication or steal session data from other players.
  • One team, one identity, one honest scoreboard position.

Communication

  • Use the official support channel for outages, broken challenges, or unclear statements.
  • Give organizers enough detail to reproduce a technical issue quickly.
  • Avoid posting spoilers in public channels during the event.

Enforcement

  • Organizers may remove solves, suspend teams, or disqualify accounts for abusive behavior.
  • If an action creates risk for the platform, the organizers may intervene immediately.
  • Final interpretation of the rules belongs to the event staff.
